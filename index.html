Welcome to Swing and Putt Golf Course

Located in the heart of Queensland's magnificent Sunshine Coast, Ray's Swing and Putt Golf Course is a jewel in the crown of the area's golf courses. An amazing design awaits you a 60 year old course with the charms and charisma that only a classic course can provide. This along with a traditional clubhouse - "cold beer and great food" - and the best views in the entire Sunshine Coast, combine to make the Ray's Swing and Putt Golf Course an unforgettable experience.
